Frequently
Asked Questions

Answers about booking, pricing, prep, aftercare, and studio policies. For anything not covered here,
send us a message.

Booking & Pricing

How do I book? ⌃
Use the “Book a Consult” form or DM the artist on Instagram. We’ll confirm availability and next steps.
Do you require a deposit? ⌃
Yes. Deposits (non-refundable) hold your spot and go toward the final price.
How much does a tattoo cost? ⌃
Pricing depends on size, placement, and detail. Small/minimum pieces start at $100; hourly rates and day rates available. Contact us for accurate pricing.
Can I get a quote online? ⌃
Rough estimates are possible with clear references, size (in inches), and placement. Final price is confirmed in person.

Design & Preparation

Can you design something custom for me? ⌃
Absolutely. Bring ideas/references; we’ll create a piece tailored to you.
Will you copy another artist’s design? ⌃
No exact copies. We’ll draw an original inspired by what you like.
Do you do cover-ups or reworks? ⌃
Yes—after a consult. High-contrast, larger designs work best for cover-ups; sometimes lightening/laser helps.
Can you tattoo over scars or stretch marks? ⌃
Often, yes—once fully healed (typically 1+ year). A consult is required.

Health & Safety

How old do I need to be? ⌃
18+ with valid, government-issued photo ID. (Adjust this if your policy differs.)
Is your shop sterile and safe? ⌃
Yes. Single-use needles, hospital-grade disinfection, and state-compliant procedures.
I have a medical condition—can I get tattooed? ⌃
Please disclose conditions/medications. Get doctor approval if unsure.
Are your inks vegan? ⌃
We use high-quality, professional-grade inks. Let us know about any allergies or sensitivities.

Aftercare & Healing

How do I take care of my tattoo? ⌃
We’ll provide written aftercare. Typically: remove wrap as directed, wash gently, pat dry, and apply a thin, fragrance-free moisturizer.
How long does healing take? ⌃
Surface heals in ~2 weeks; full healing is ~6–8 weeks.
When can I swim, tan, or work out? ⌃
No swimming, hot tubs, or saunas for 2–3 weeks. Avoid sun exposure while healing.
Do you offer touch-ups? ⌃
Yes. One complimentary touch-up within 6-12 months for normal healing (excludes hands/feet/finger tips and neglect).
