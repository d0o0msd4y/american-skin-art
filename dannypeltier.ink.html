Danny Peltier

@dannypeltier.ink

Danny Peltier (@dannypeltier.ink) brings a sharp eye for clean linework and thoughtful composition to the studio. Specializing in a mix of illustrative, neo-traditional, and pop-culture-inspired pieces, Danny is known for creating readable designs that hold up over time. Whether you want a tribute to your favorite movie or a bold custom design, Danny focuses on art that stays fun for the long haul.
